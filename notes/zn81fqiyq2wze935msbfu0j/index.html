Integrating Products of Powers of Sin and Cos

Now that we know Integrating Powers of sin x and cos x, we can apply the half-angle formulas to integrate products of powers of sin and cos.

Example

  1. ∫sin⁡4xcos⁡2x dx\int{\sin^4{x}\cos^2{x}}\,dx∫sin4xcos2xdx
  2. ∫sin⁡3xcos⁡−2x dx\int{\sin^3{x}\cos^{-2}{x}}\,dx∫sin3xcos−2xdx
