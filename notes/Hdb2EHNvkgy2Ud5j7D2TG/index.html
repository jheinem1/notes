Trigonometric Functions

The trigonometric functions are sin⁡x\sin{x}sinx , cos⁡x\cos{x}cosx , tan⁡x\tan{x}tanx , cot⁡x\cot{x}cotx , sec⁡x\sec{x}secx , and csc⁡x\csc{x}cscx ; they are fundamental to mathematics and many areas of application. Also important are their relatives, the Inverse Trigonometric Functions.
