Sequence of Partial Sums

The sequence of partial sums of a sequence Sn{S_n}Sn​ is defined as

S1,S2,…,Sn,{S_1, S_2, \ldots, S_n}\text,S1​,S2​,…,Sn​,

where

Sn=x1+x2+⋯+xn.S_n=x_1+x_2+\cdots+x_n\text.Sn​=x1​+x2​+⋯+xn​.

∑k=1∞xk\sum_{k=1}^\infty{x_k}k=1∑∞​xk​

lim⁡k→∞xk=∑k=1∞xk.\lim_{k\to\infty}{x_k}=\sum_{k=1}^{\infty}{x_k}\text.k→∞lim​xk​=k=1∑∞​xk​.
