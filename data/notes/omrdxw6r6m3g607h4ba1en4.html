Example

Evaluate ∫tan⁡4x dx\int{\tan^4{x}}\,dx∫tan4xdx.

Solution

Reduction formula 3 gives

∫tan4x dx=13tan⁡3x−∫tan2x dx\int{tan^4{x}}\,dx=\frac13\tan^3{x}-\int{tan^2{x}}\,dx∫tan4xdx=31​tan3x−∫tan2xdx
=13tan⁡3x−(tan⁡x−∫tan⁡0x dx)=\frac13\tan^3{x}-(\tan{x}-\int{\tan^0{x}}\,dx)=31​tan3x−(tanx−∫tan0xdx)
=13tan⁡3x−tan⁡x+x+C.=\frac13\tan^3{x}-\tan{x}+x+C\text.=31​tan3x−tanx+x+C.

An alternative solution uses the identity tan2x=sec2x−1tan^2{x}=sec^2{x}-1tan2x=sec2x−1:

∫tan⁡4x dx=∫tan⁡2x(sec⁡2x−1) dx\int{\tan^4{x}}\,dx=\int{\tan^2{x}(\sec^2{x}-1)}\,dx∫tan4xdx=∫tan2x(sec2x−1)dx

The substitution u=tan⁡xu=\tan{x}u=tanx, du=sec⁡2xdu=\sec^2{x}du=sec2x is used in the first integral, while the identity tan⁡2x=sec2x−1\tan^2{x}=sec^2{x}-1tan2x=sec2x−1 is used in the second integral:

∫tan4x dx=∫tan⁡2xsec⁡2x dx−∫tan⁡2x dx\int{tan^4{x}}\,dx=\int{\tan^2{x}\sec^2{x}}\,dx-\int{\tan^2{x}}\,dx∫tan4xdx=∫tan2xsec2xdx−∫tan2xdx
=∫u2 du−∫(sec⁡2x−1) dx=\int{u^2}\,du-\int{(\sec^2{x}-1)}\,dx=∫u2du−∫(sec2x−1)dx
=u33−tan⁡x+x+C=\frac{u^3}{3}-\tan{x}+x+C=3u3​−tanx+x+C
