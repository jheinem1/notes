Integrating Products of Powers of Tan and Sec

Integrals of the for ∫tan⁡mxsec⁡nx dx\int{\tan^m{x}\sec^n{x}}\,dx∫tanmxsecnxdx are evaluated using methods analogous to those used for ∫sin⁡mx dx\int{\sin^m{x}}\,dx∫sinmxdx.

For example, if nnn is an even positive integer, we split off a factor of sec⁡2x\sec^2{x}sec2x and write the remaining even power of sec⁡x\sec{x}secx in terms of tan⁡x\tan{x}tanx. If mmm is odd, we split off a factor of sec⁡xtan⁡x\sec{x}\tan{x}secxtanx, which prepares the integral for the substitution u=sec⁡xu=\sec{x}u=secx. If mmm is even and nnn is odd, the integrand is expressed as a polynomial in sec⁡x\sec{x}secx, each of whose terms are handled by a reduction formula.
