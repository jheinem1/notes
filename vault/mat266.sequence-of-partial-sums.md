The sequence of partial sums of a sequence ${S_n}$ is defined as

$$
{S_1, S_2, \ldots, S_n}\text,
$$

where

$$
S_n=x_1+x_2+\cdots+x_n\text.
$$

This can be used to evaluate the (infinite) series

$$
\sum_{k=1}^\infty{x_k}
$$

with the limit

$$
\lim_{k\to\infty}{x_k}=\sum_{k=1}^{\infty}{x_k}\text.
$$
