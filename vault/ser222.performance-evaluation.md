1. What is the Big-Oh of `isEmpty()`?
    - `O(1)`
2. What is the Big-Oh of `pop()`?
    - `O(1)`
3. What is the Big-Oh of `push()`?
    - `O(n)`, because the worst case involve array resizing (which performs a deep copy of the array)
