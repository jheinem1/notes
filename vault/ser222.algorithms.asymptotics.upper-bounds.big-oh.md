- $f(n)=O(g(n))$ indicates that $f(n)$ has an upper bound
- To find $g(n)$ for $f(n)$ in $f(n)=O(g(n))$, we take $f(n)$, eliminate all but the dominant term, and remove any constant factor from the dominant term
